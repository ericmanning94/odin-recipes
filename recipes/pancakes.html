Pancakes

Description

A yummy and easy way to start the day

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup protein powder
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Add all the dry ingredients to a bowl and mix until consistent.
  2. Add the cup of water to the dry ingredients and mix until saturated, add more if needed. Batter should be somewhat runny
  3. Heat a pan on the stove top at medium heat and add butter or neutral cooking oil and allow to warm up
  4. Add 1/4 cup of pancake batter to oil and fry until bubbles start to appear on the surface. At this moment, flip the pancake. It should be golden brown
  5. Cook the other side until golden brown, set aside to service
  6. Serve warm with fresh fruit, butter, and syrup
